VIDEO BACKGROUND -- 
  video tag -
    the video tag is used to embed video content in a document, it cointains a <source> subtag 
    which can contain one or more sources, the browser will display the first source it 
    supports
  video tag attributes -
    autoplay - Video plays as soon as it is ready.
    loop - Video runs in loop.
    muted - the output of the video is muted.
    poster - display an image until the video is loading or until the user hits the play button.
